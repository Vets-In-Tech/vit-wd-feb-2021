ChiyaUp

We all need support or at least someone to talk to, but sometimes we either don't have that someone, or what is bothering us is not easy to express. We at ChiyaUp understand this so we have created a safe space in which you can pour all your emotions anonymously. After you're done writing how you feel, we suggest you read what you just wrote to understand how you are feeling. When you're ready, press the release button to delete this text, release all the negative emotions (if any) and make today a great day!
