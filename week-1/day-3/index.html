Box Model

  • margin
  • border
  • padding
  • content

Content: is the text, image or video that you are displaying

Padding: is the space around the content (think bubble wrap)

Border: is the surrounding area next to the padding (think cardboard box)

Margin: is the area surrounding the border (think space between boxes)
